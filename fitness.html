Fitness Coaching

Fitness coaching focused on strength, discipline, endurance, and consistency as important foundations for physical and mental development. During our activities, I also had the opportunity to go to the gym with my friends where I helped coach them and guide them through proper workout routines. I assisted in teaching them the correct forms of lifting weights to avoid injury and ensure that each movement was done safely and effectively. This included demonstrating proper posture, breathing techniques, and controlled movements during exercises. Through this experience, I learned the importance of discipline and consistency not only for myself but also when helping others improve their fitness journey. I observed how proper guidance can greatly improve performance and prevent common mistakes in training. It also helped me develop leadership skills, patience, and communication when explaining techniques to others. Overall, this experience strengthened my understanding of fitness training and deepened my passion for helping others achieve their health and strength goals.
